BRIYANI

Biryani, a well-liked meal that initially originated from India, has now become famous across the globe. It consists of rice and is mixed with either meat or vegetables, along with seasoning and herbs to create a savory and fragrant taste.Biryani is commonly presented during important events and get-togethers, making it a fundamental part of numerous traditions. Its substantial historical background and delightful flavor have established it as a popular dish among food aficionados and social media users.
